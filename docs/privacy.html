Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Michael D. Callaghan ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Park Pursuit Bingo mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Park Pursuit Bingo is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Game Data: Your BINGO cards, game progress, and settings are stored locally on your device only.
  • No Account Required: You don't need to create an account or provide personal information to use Park Pursuit Bingo.
  • No Location Tracking: We do not track your location or require GPS access.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard mobile storage APIs. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it using the app's sharing features.

Third-Party Services

Park Pursuit Bingo may include:

  • Amazon Affiliate Links: We participate in the Amazon Associates program. When you click on product recommendations and make purchases, we may earn a commission. Amazon's privacy policy governs data collected through their platform.
  • App Store Services: The app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play) may collect usage analytics according to their privacy policies.

Sharing Features

When you use the "Share" feature, Park Pursuit Bingo generates an image or text summary of your game progress. This data is shared using your device's native sharing functionality to the apps and services you choose.

Children's Privacy

Park Pursuit Bingo is a family-friendly app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require account creation or personal data input.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using device passcodes and keeping your operating system updated.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete all game data by using the "Clear All Game Data" option in Settings or by uninstalling the app.
  • Your data is never uploaded to our servers, so there's nothing for us to delete.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
